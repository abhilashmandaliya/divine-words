### Date and Time

10-11-2010 Wednesday 05:20 PM

### Title

JASHNN E ROOH

#### Poem

AHAMM KI AAG SE . . . GUJARKAR . . . JANA HAI . . . HAMNE ZAGMAGAATE . . . SATYA KO ! REHAM KI RAAH SE JIVANBHAR . . . MEETTAA GAYA HUN . . . MILTE HI SAI KO ! EK EK LAMHA HUA EEBAADAT MERI . . . SHUKRAGUJAAR HUN . . . PAAKAR TERI EEZAZAT KO ! . . . TASAWWUR TERA BAN GAYA TISHNAGEE MERI ! ! ! AB TO HAI SALAAM PARWARDIGAAR KO ! . . . ROOTHA KOI , CHHUTA KOI . CHHUT GAI PARWAAH ! . . . RABB MILA . . . KHUDAHAAFIZ SAB KO ! JASHNN E ROOH HAI MERE ALLAH . . . ! PEETA HUN . . . PEELATA HUN . . . TERE NOOR KO ! ASHQK BHI AATE HAI OOBHARKAR TO , NACHTE . . . GAATE . . . SHUKRIYA KE GEET KO ! TERI REHMAT KO BAYAN KAISE KARU , MERE MAALIK TU HI TU NAZAR AATA HAI MUJKO ! . . . HAR ALFAAZ AATA NAMAAZ BANKAR . . . YA. . RABB TERI DUAA MILE SAB KO . . . ! ! !
